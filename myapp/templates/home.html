Tutorial: Getting Started

  1. Authentication and Account Creation

    1. Visit the Website: Open your web browser and navigate to the Online Resume Watcher website.
    2. Create an Account: Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button to create a new account. Provide your email address and create a secure password.
    3. Log In: After registering, you'll be redirected to the login page. Enter your email and password, then click "Log In" to access your personalized dashboard.

  2. Upload Your Resume

    1. Access Resume Section: From your dashboard, click on the "Upload Resume" or "Add New Resume" button.
    2. Choose Your Resume File: Click on the "Choose File" button and select your resume in PDF format from your computer. You can also provide a title or description for your resume.
    3. Upload Your Resume: After selecting the file and providing any additional details, click "Upload" or "Save." Your resume is now uploaded to the platform.

  3. View Others' Resumes Without Login

    1. Access Shared Resume URL: To view someone else's resume without logging in, obtain their shared resume URL. This URL might be shared with you directly or through a job application.
    2. Open the Shared URL: Copy and paste the shared resume URL into your web browser's address bar. Press "Enter," and the resume will be displayed directly on the website without requiring a login or file download.

  4. Reupload an Updated Resume Without Changing Your URL

    1. Access Your Dashboard: Log in to your Online Resume Watcher account and access your personalized dashboard.
    2. Edit Your Resume: Find the resume you want to update and click on the "Edit" or "Update" button next to it.
    3. Choose the Updated File: Click on the "Choose File" button and select the updated version of your resume in PDF format from your computer.
    4. Update Your Resume: After selecting the new file, click "Update" or "Save." The platform will automatically replace the old resume file with the updated version while keeping the same personalized URL intact.
